1. Components:
- Synchronization
- Aggregation

2. Interactions:
- Aggregation → Synchronization : subscribeStage
- Synchronization → Aggregation : onStageEvent

3. Execution flow:
- The Aggregation-side StageEventListener attaches to the Synchronization-side StageEventBridge by calling subscribeStage, registering its onStageEvent as a slot. 
- Later, StageEventBridge.publishStage is invoked; because a listener is connected, the bridge emits its boost::signals2 signal. 
- This runtime signal dispatch invokes StageEventListener.onStageEvent in the Aggregation component, as confirmed by the trace showing Synchronization → Aggregation : onStageEvent.
