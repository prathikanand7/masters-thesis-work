1) Components

- Synchronization
- Logging

2) Interactions
- Aggregation → Logging : RAIILogStream.stream
- Synchronization → Aggregation : Aggregator.getAll
- Synchronization → Logging : CPSLogger.flush

3) Execution flow
- Two aggregators are populated with runnable objects; each Aggregator.add call emits logging via RAIILogStream.stream from Aggregation to Logging (four times total in the trace). 
- Two SynchronizedRunner threads start and, within runSynchronized, each fetches runnable objects by calling Aggregator.getAll (two calls observed). 
- As the runners progress through their synchronized stages, they repeatedly call CPSLogger::flush to synchronize output, producing multiple Synchronization → Logging flush interactions. 
- The runners continue this cycle until completion, as indicated by the repeated flush events.
