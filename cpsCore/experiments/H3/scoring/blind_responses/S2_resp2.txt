3) Execution flow
- Methods in Configuration::PropertyMapper (add, addEnum, addEigen, addVector, getChild, mandatoryCheck) are invoked in sequence. 
- Each of these methods emits a log via the CPSLOG_* macros, which construct a RAIILogStream and call its stream method in the Logging component.
- The runtime trace records seven consecutive invocations of Logging::RAIILogStream.stream originating from those PropertyMapper methods. 
- When keys are present, add/addEnum log trace messages; when mandatory keys are missing, getChild/mandatoryCheck log errors, all via the same RAIILogStream.stream interface.
